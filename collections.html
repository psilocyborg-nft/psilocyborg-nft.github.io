Wellspring

As seen on:

>

A cyclic font of cyber hallucinations. This collection consists of animations produced using generative algorithms (VQGAN+CLIP) based on a variety of text prompts to create an infinite loop. The text prompts are from the world of music and philosophy such as album titles by Pink Floyd, The Beatles, and even the works of Nietzsche.

Each work can be displayed looping forwards or backwards, could be set to a single frame, or slowed down to display a new frame every minute, hour, or day.

Below are a hand picked selection of individual frames, view the collection on OpenSea to see them as animations.

Cyber Dreamer

Have you ever wondered how an AI would see the world? The Dreamer Collection brings you the how. Each piece in this collections is based on a photograph taken by the artist, with the DeepDream algorithm applied to it to produce the psychedelic effect.

The Infinite Plane

Experiments in tessellation. This collection will explore periodic and non-periodic tilings with cyclic, animated embellishments.

Ceci n'est pas une

The title of this collection is inspired by the painting "Ceci n'est pas une pipe" by Rene Magritte, who points out that no matter how naturalistically we depict an object, we never do catch the item itself. The items in this collection are produced via the classic Generative Adversarial Network (GAN), DCGAN, which learns to convert random sequences of numbers into realistic images. The cats in this collection never existed, thus NonCats is born!.
